Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for businesses in Hyderabad, AP & Telangana

Do you provide digital marketing services in Hyderabad?

add

Yes. We provide full-stack digital marketing in Hyderabad including SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn, content strategy and analytics. Our team also supports Nellore, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Tirupati and remote clients across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

How much does franchise expansion consulting cost in Andhra Pradesh?

add

Franchise expansion projects are scoped by brand size, number of units and rollout geography. A typical engagement for an AP or Telangana brand begins with a paid discovery workshop; long-term mandates are quoted per-unit or as a monthly retainer. Reach out via the contact form for a free 30-minute consultation and written estimate.

Are you MSME / Udyam registered?

add

Yes. Digi Empower India is registered as an MSME under the Government of India's Udyam Registration system. We can share our Udyam Registration Number and MSME certificate on request for enterprise procurement and on-boarding paperwork.

Which cities do you serve across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh?

add

On-ground delivery is currently focused on Hyderabad, Nellore, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam and Tirupati. Digital services, legal & tax filings, and advisory engagements are delivered remotely across all of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, and also to Indian clients operating overseas.

Do you offer free software training?

add

Yes. As part of our community commitment we run free software training programs on productivity suites, analytics tools and digital marketing fundamentals. Sessions are announced on our LinkedIn and Instagram pages; contact us for the current schedule.

Can you help with legal and tax filings for an AP-based business?

add

Yes. We support GST registration and filing, MSME/Udyam registration, GSTIN compliance, ROC filings, and ongoing corporate legal framework for businesses headquartered in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and beyond.
